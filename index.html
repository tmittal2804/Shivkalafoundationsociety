Who We Are

A Legacy of Compassion
and Community Action

Founded in 1988 in the sacred city of Haridwar, Uttarakhand, Shiv Kala Foundation Society has spent over three decades building bridges between need and opportunity. What began as a small community initiative has grown into a structured organization touching thousands of lives each year.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through inclusive programs that create lasting social change.

Our Vision

An inclusive society where every individual has access to dignity, education, and opportunity.

What We Do

Programs Built Around
Real Community Needs

Each initiative is designed with community members, not just for them — because lasting change starts from within.

Child Education

Bridging learning gaps for underprivileged children through remedial classes, learning kits, and scholarship support — because every child deserves a fair start.

Learn More
Women Empowerment

Skill development, legal awareness, self-help groups, and microfinance support that help women reclaim agency and build economic independence.

Learn More
Senior Citizen Welfare

Health camps, companionship programs, and dignity-first services ensuring our elders live with respect, care, and a strong social support network.

Learn More
Community Development

Infrastructure, sanitation, and civic engagement projects co-designed with local residents to create healthier, more connected neighbourhoods.

Learn More
Volunteer Initiatives

Structured volunteer programs that train and deploy passionate citizens in meaningful roles across all our focus areas throughout Uttarakhand.

Learn More
Environmental Awareness

Tree plantation drives, river clean-ups along the Ganga, and school awareness campaigns protecting the natural heritage of Haridwar for future generations.

Why Choose Us

Accountability You
Can Measure

We believe donors, volunteers, and communities deserve full transparency. Every programme is documented, every rupee tracked, and every outcome reported — openly.

Experienced Team

Over 35 years of on-ground experience across diverse social welfare domains in the Uttarakhand region.

Community Driven

All programmes are co-designed with and for the communities we serve — ensuring relevance and real ownership.

Transparent Operations

Annual reports, fund utilisation statements, and programme audits available to all stakeholders.

Sustainable Impact

We invest in long-term solutions — building skills, systems, and community capacity, not just short-term relief.
