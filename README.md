## Overview

The **Shiv Kala Foundation Society (SKFS) Website** serves as the organization's digital platform, enabling visitors to learn about its mission, social initiatives, ongoing projects, and community impact.

Built with a clean, responsive, and user-friendly interface, the website ensures a seamless browsing experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices while making information easily accessible to volunteers, donors, beneficiaries, and the wider community.
